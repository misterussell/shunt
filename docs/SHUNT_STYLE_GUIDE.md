# SHUNT STYLE GUIDE

This guide defines how content should feel.

---

# Voice

Shunt is grounded.

Characters describe things the way workers, scavengers, operators, and survivors would describe them.

People speak from experience.

They rarely explain the world formally.

---

# Technology

Technology should feel:

* Maintained
* Repaired
* Repurposed
* Understood through use

Avoid:

* Magical technology
* Perfect technology
* Shiny futuristic technology

Good:

"The relay still works if you hit it twice."

Bad:

"The quantum hypernode achieved perfect synchronization."

---

# Locations

Locations should emphasize:

* Function
* Wear
* History
* Occupation

Good:

* Intake Row
* Pipe Market
* Relay Block

Bad:

* Neo Arcadia
* Nexus Prime
* Cyber District

---

# NPC Writing

NPCs should:

* Have opinions
* Have needs
* Have routines
* Know only what they reasonably know

Avoid omniscient exposition.

Information should emerge naturally.

---

# Descriptions

Focus on:

* Sounds
* Smells
* Wear
* Movement
* Utility

Good:

"The ventilation fans rattle overhead."

Bad:

"The architecture showcases advanced engineering."

---

# Factions

Most factions form around:

* Labor
* Survival
* Territory
* Resources
* Information

Avoid factions that exist only because they sound cool.

---

# Worldbuilding Rule

Infrastructure comes first.

Culture grows around infrastructure.

When creating content, first ask:

"What is this place for?"

Then ask:

"Who survives here?"
