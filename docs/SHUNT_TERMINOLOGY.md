# SHUNT TERMINOLOGY

This document defines canonical terminology used throughout Shunt.

The purpose is consistency, not completeness.

If a term appears here, it should generally be preferred over synonyms.

---

# Core Themes

Shunt is a salvaged future.

Technology is old, repurposed, repaired, and layered upon itself.

People live inside infrastructure.

Most technology feels industrial rather than sleek.

Language should feel practical, grounded, and working-class.

---

# Technology

## Latticework

The distributed network layer connecting systems throughout the world.

Preferred Usage:

* Access the Latticework
* Trace a signal through the Latticework
* Lost in the Latticework

Avoid:

* Internet
* Cyberspace
* Matrix
* Metaverse

---

## Ghostdeck

Portable interface device used to interact with systems connected to the Latticework.

Preferred Usage:

* Slot in your Ghostdeck
* Scan with a Ghostdeck
* Connect your Ghostdeck

---

# Skills

## Ghostwork

Interacting with the Latticework.

Includes:

* Hacking
* Signal tracing
* ICE-breaking
* Data recovery

---

## The Web

Building leverage through relationships, favors, secrets, and social connections.

Includes:

* Calling favors
* Gathering information
* Managing contacts
* Social influence

# People

## Fixer

Information broker, deal maker, or intermediary.

---

## Runner

Individual who performs dangerous work for pay.

# Infrastructure

## Relay

Network-connected device, station, or junction.

---

## Node

A significant connected location within the world.

---

# Common Slang

## Burned

Compromised, exposed, or unusable.

## Ghosted

Disconnected, vanished, or intentionally hidden.

## Spliced

Modified, patched, or improvised.

## Cold

Inactive, disconnected, or abandoned.

---

# Terminology Goals

Prefer:

* Industrial terms
* Infrastructure terms
* Utility-focused language

Avoid:

* Fantasy terminology
* Corporate marketing language
* Excessively futuristic buzzwords
